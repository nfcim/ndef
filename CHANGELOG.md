## 0.1.0

* Initial release, preliminary decoding & encoding support
* Support types:
  * NFC Well Known
    * Text
    * URI with well-known prefix
    * Digital signature
    * Smart poster
    * Connection handover
  * Media (MIME data)
    * Bluetooth easy pairing / Bluetooth low energy
    * other MIME data
  * Absolute URI

## 0.2.0
